# Shared Vault Templates

## Idea
Borrowing template ideas from the community is useful, but adapt them to your metadata rules and actual workflows.

## Good Sources of Reusable Structure
- Project dashboards
- Literature note layouts
- Weekly and monthly review prompts
- Research maps and MOC layouts

## Adoption Rule
Import the structure, not the clutter. If a section is not useful in practice, remove it.
