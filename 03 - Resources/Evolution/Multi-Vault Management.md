# Multi-Vault Management

## Idea
Use separate vaults only when context, permissions, or workflow boundaries are truly different.

## Good Split Criteria
- Personal versus team knowledge
- Highly different domains or privacy needs
- Performance limits in a very large vault

## Keep Shared Standards
- Similar templates
- Similar metadata keys
- A clear export or sync path between vaults
