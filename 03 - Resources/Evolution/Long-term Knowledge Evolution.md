# Long-term Knowledge Evolution

## Idea
The goal is not to store more notes, but to make the note network increasingly coherent, generative, and reusable.

## Signs of Healthy Evolution
- More evergreen notes and fewer stranded captures
- Better cross-links between domains
- Stronger MOCs and bridge notes
- Faster retrieval of old insights
