# Capture - Process - Connect

## Capture
Send rough notes, highlights, and ideas into `00 - Inbox/` or `06 - Knowledge/Fleeting Notes/` with minimal friction.

## Process
Clarify the note type, add frontmatter, extract key ideas, and move the note into the right PARA location.

## Connect
Add wikilinks, suggest related notes, and update relevant MOCs so the note joins the broader system.

## Claude Use
Best command: `/process-inbox` followed by `/find-connections`.
