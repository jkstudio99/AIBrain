# Literature Notes

## Goal
Capture source material in your own words so it becomes usable, linkable, and ready for synthesis.

## Workflow
1. Create from `Templates/Literature Note`.
2. Record source metadata.
3. Summarize key ideas and reactions.
4. Mark candidate evergreen notes.
5. Link the note into a MOC or research cluster.

## Quality Check
- One note per source
- Summary is paraphrased, not copied
- At least one question or implication is captured
