# Evergreen Notes

## Goal
Evergreen notes hold atomic ideas written clearly enough to stay useful over time.

## Workflow
1. Start from a literature note, fleeting note, or synthesis.
2. Reduce the material to one durable idea.
3. Write a declarative title.
4. Add supporting evidence and links.
5. Grow the note from `seedling` to `evergreen`.

## Quality Check
- One idea only
- Clear claim in your own words
- Connected to at least two other notes
