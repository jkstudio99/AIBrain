# Research & Synthesis

## Goal
Move from collected material to integrated understanding and output.

## Workflow
1. Gather sources and create literature notes.
2. Group notes by theme or tension.
3. Use synthesis prompts to combine ideas.
4. Create bridge notes or evergreen notes.
5. Update the relevant MOC.
