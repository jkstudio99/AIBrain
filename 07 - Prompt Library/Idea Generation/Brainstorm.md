# 💡 Brainstorm

## Prompt
```
Brainstorm ideas around this topic:

[TOPIC/QUESTION]

Rules:
- Generate 10+ diverse ideas (quantity over quality first)
- Include at least 2 "wild card" unconventional ideas
- Mix practical and ambitious ideas
- For each idea, add one sentence on WHY it could work
- Star the top 3 most promising ideas

Then organize into: Quick Wins | Medium Effort | Moonshots
```
