# 🌙 Daily Reflection

## Prompt
```
Help me reflect on my day. Here are my notes:

[PASTE TODAY'S NOTES/CAPTURES]

Guide me through:
1. **Harvest**: What are the key insights or learnings from today?
2. **Patterns**: What patterns do I notice (recurring themes, emotions, blockers)?
3. **Connections**: How do today's experiences connect to ongoing projects or goals?
4. **Growth**: What stretched me today? What was comfortable?
5. **Tomorrow**: Based on today, what should I prioritize tomorrow?

Keep the reflection concise and actionable.
```
